Welcome. You have found us!

This adventure started more than a year ago. For quite some time, we have managed large couponing portals in Europe. But we felt we could still add more to the shopping experience. Don't you agree? Online shopping can be much more effective - cheaper and much more fun! This is exactly our ambition: to create a global couponing environment which adapts to your wishes and needs perfectly.

How do we make this happen? Well, you decide. It might result in a geo-based couponing App for Google Glass, or a postal service that delivers customized coupons to your door. What we do know is that our developers are ready to build exactly what you want based on your reviews and feedback.

We'll be launching in many countries in the near future, we want to help the entire world saving as much on online shopping. Become part of our journey. Use our website and give us your feedback.

E-mail us for more info, our presskit or any questions about this adventure!
